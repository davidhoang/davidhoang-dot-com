By Design Podcast Brought to You by Abstract

April 17, 2021

I had the pleasure of being on an episode of the By Design podcast, hosted by Abstract’s co-founder Josh Brewer. Check out the amazing list of guests who appears—have been learning so much from the others.

  • ‍Sana Rao on research: let your questions be your guide
  • ‍David Sherwin and Mary Paynter Sherwin on the product brief: find alignment
  • ‍Marc Hemeon on exploration: constraints unlock creativity
  • ‍David Hoang on prototyping: failing faster is better
  • ‍Katie Dill on reviews: how to make them more inclusive and productive
  • ‍Joshua Goldenberg on building & shipping: start collaborating early
  • ‍Fonz Morris on refinement: data is your friend
  • ‍Evan English on outcomes and design maturity: design teams need to be nurtured
