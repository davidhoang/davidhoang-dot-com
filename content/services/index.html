David will consult on a limited basis as a fractional executive focused on Design, Brand, and Marketing. He can help with scaling startups as a strategic partner or acting operator.

What is fractional leadership?

Whether you're a small startup or a large company, fractional leadership is a way to help you grow and scale. Fractional leadership is a flexible model that ensures you're getting value as needed and not over-scaling the company when it's not necessary.

David can act as an interim executive and brings his experience as an operator spanning from Design, Brand, Marketing, and Executive leadership. He provides fresh perspectives, operational best practices, mentorship and skill development, cross-functional collaboration, and valuable guidance to enhance the overall design capabilities and outcomes of the organization.
