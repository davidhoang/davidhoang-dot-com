About David

I'm an operator, investor, and writer.

My passion is to help people build their ideas and achieve their dreams though software creation. I'm a startup operator, investor, and writer.

I currently work at Replit as the VP of Marketing and Design. Previously, was Head of Design at Webflow, One Medical, and Black Pixel, and a co-founder of a software studio called Xhatch Interactive.

I love teaching and had the pleasure of guest lecturing about design at institutions such as the University of Washington, Duke, and Hyper Island. I used to teach UX at General Assembly and a Design Partner for On Deck's Design Fellowship program.
